My Philippine Instagram Pictures



by Jack Northrup -I have taken several pictures while in the Philippines and have shared them on instagram. My Philippine Instagram pictures var from the Philippine countryside to some of the digital graphics I do in photoshop. A lot are of Philippine marbled paper designs and graphics created from them. I came over here to retire but have decided to keep working and start a marble paper business for Myra. I have created websites for several years and and will use what I have learned to create a couple sites SEO friendly to try and sell the marbles either in lots or as digital downloads. I will also have CDs of the marbled papers we make available for purchase either as tangible item or as a digital download.  

I have several videos on youtube you may wish too watch on "Life in the Philippines'. Many videos are on life in the Philippines including trips to town and trips to the market to purchase Carrageenana seaweed. You also can watch the "learning to marble" videos that have turned into a business. We use real seaweed (Carrageenana) cooked to create the sizing used in marbled paper. Sizing is the liquid that paints float on in order to creat marbled paper. We use ox gall collected at the slaugherhouse as a paint additive to make the paints spead on the sizing surface. We make our own paint base. If you wish to see marbled papers being made t.Take time to watch our YOUTUBE VIDEOS   I hope you will find them intertaining as well as educational.
